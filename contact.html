Contacting Me

Please read the following questions before contacting me privately! I read all messages but can only respond to a small number (otherwise I'd spend all day responding to messages and we'd no longer have any competitions on Kaggle!). Also, be aware that I travel a fair amount and may not see your message within 48 hours of when you send it.

  • Are you a recruiter?
    I'm sorry but I'm not interested. However, I recommend posting the job on Kaggle's Jobs Board.
  • Do you have a question pertaining to a specific Kaggle competition?
    If so, please ask the question on the competition's corresponding forums. A representative from the host organization will see it there.
  • Do you urgently need a Kaggle employee to be aware of an issue?
    If so, please read Kaggle's Support FAQ. If that doesn't answer your question, email support at kaggle.com.
  • Are you trying to sell an enterprise technology or service?
    Sorry, not interested.
  • I'm interested in machine learning / data science! How do I learn?
    I need to create a blog post on this. For now, I recommend the links under "Learning Data Science" on Kaggle's wiki.
